    • Sharetribe is the easiest way to create your own peer-to-peer marketplace website in the spirit of Airbnb, Fiverr or Etsy.

    In Sharetribe marketplaces, members can rent or sell products, services or spaces to each other. Our platform enables anyone to create such a site without coding skills. Setting the site up is super easy and only takes a couple of minutes.

    The Sharetribe platform powers numerous marketplaces in 15 countries. Our mission is to make it accessible and affordable for anyone around the world to create their own marketplace site.

    Our company is based in Helsinki, Finland and currently employs 9 people.
